Step 1: Discover my business stage

In this step you’ll find a test to determine in which development stage your business is. It’s extremely important to know in which phase your company is:

(1) You need to know where you stand to anticipate potential challenges & obstacles.

(2) You need to adapt your funding strategy in order to increase chances to get funded.

Before your start, one important tip:

It’s very important to be extremely honest about:

(1) your business strengths and weaknesses

(2) your vision

(3) the market solutions you are providing

Only in this way you will be able to understand where your business is and what you need to develop your green business.

Keep this in mind and good luck !
